about me

This guy in the picture, that's me. I'm Donald and I develop computer things you may like. Coming from a business management background, I reached into the world of computer programming with a desire to stretch my brain to its limits and then push past those self-imposed limits and learn some more.

I self taught myself for about 6 months, trying to soak up bits of knowledge all over, learning a lot - but struggling to find a set direction. That led me to PDX Code Guild in Portland, OR where I enrolled and completed a Python based Full Stack Development Course.
